About Me

Frontend Developer | Designer & 3D Artist | Creative Problem-Solver

I’m Mohamed Guled, a frontend developer recently graduated from Hyper Island, where I focused on building interactive and user-centered web experiences. I mainly work with JavaScript, React, and TypeScript, and enjoy creating interfaces that are both intuitive and well-structured.

During my internship at bio.se, I worked on features that are live today, contributing across the full process from UX thinking and structure to implementation. I’m comfortable working close to both design and development, and I enjoy turning ideas into real, usable products.

Alongside development, I have a background in graphic design and 3D, which has given me a strong eye for detail and how things actually feel to use. I also use modern tools like Cursor and Claude to improve how I work and move faster when building and solving problems.

I also have experience working with communication and teamwork through my time with WWF Sweden Youth, and currently I work with Stockholm Stad’s youth services in Järva. There, I run programming workshops and support young people with school and job preparation, which has strengthened my ability to explain technical concepts and collaborate with different people.
