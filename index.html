About Me

Frontend Developer (in the making) | Designer & 3D Artist | Creative Problem-Solver

I’m Mohamed Guled, a junior frontend developer currently sharpening my skills at Hyper Island, where I’m learning to build interactive, user-centric web experiences. But I’m not just about code—I’m a self-taught graphic designer and 3D artist who thrives on turning ideas into visuals.

By day, I’m deep in JavaScript and React; by night, I freelance design projects and craft 3D renders that blend realism with imagination. Whether it’s a sleek website interface, a bold brand identity, or a surreal 3D scene, I love bridging the gap between functionality and aesthetic impact.

Beyond the screen, I have a background in communication and project management, having worked with WWF Sweden Youth on initiatives centered around environmental sustainability and social inclusion. This experience shaped my collaborative mindset and deepened my commitment to using tech and design for positive impact.

Currently, I’m also working with Stockholm Stad’s youth services in Järva, supporting 16–19-year-olds as they transition into adulthood. I run programming and IT workshops, help with school work, job searching, and organize activities that empower and inspire. It’s a space where I bring both my technical knowledge and my passion for mentorship to the table.
